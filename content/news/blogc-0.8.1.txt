TITLE: [ANN] blogc-0.8.1
DATE: 2016-04-17 02:30:00
-------------------------
I just released version ``0.8.1`` of blogc. This release fixes a bug when
using \-\- and \-\-\- inside inline code. The dashes should not be
converted to ``&ndash;`` and ``&mdash;`` in such cases.

For download links, please visit [[https://blogc.rgm.io]].
Windows users can find prebuilt binaries in the web page sidebar.

Thanks,  
Rafael
